Hi my name is Cecille and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

Embrace confusion. It means your brain has recognized that something different is happening...your brain is now at attention.

Picture yourself standing at the side of a pool...now jump in!

Trust in the resources you already have under your belt.

You don't have to know everything (of course). Just start with what you do know, including the books, article, websites you know about (like Google!) and have at it.

Oh, and by the way, you probably have made a few friends that could probably help you out as well.
